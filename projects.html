Online Breakdown Assistance System (2025)

Breakdowns on the road often leave vehicle owners stranded, creating stress and delays while searching for immediate assistance. This mini project was developed to provide a reliable, technology‑driven solution that connects users with nearby workshops and service providers in real time. The system is designed to minimize response time and improve transparency.

Disaster Management System DMS (2025–2026)

In the face of increasing frequency and intensity of disasters worldwide, an effective Disaster Management System (DMS) becomes indispensable. This project introduces a robust and integrated DMS tailored to provide timely assistance and streamline communication during crises. It offers emergency helpline access, real‑time alerts, volunteer registration, and citizen communication platforms. By combining technology with community participation, this DMS enhances disaster resilience and minimizes loss of life and property.
